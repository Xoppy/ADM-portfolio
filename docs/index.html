Pricing

Wordpress website

This is my lowest price range, I will build a WordPress based website for you or your business. This kind of websites are designed to be frequently updated with content.
They are mostly suited for blogs but can also be used for personal pages or e-commerce shops.

Price range : 80€ - 100€

Contact me

Standard integration

This price range is the most common, I will take a website mockup that you will send me and turn it into an actual website, please note that I won't work on the visual part of the project.
This option is most suited for businesses landing pages, static custom pages and project portfolios.

Price range : 300€ - 500€

Contact me

Freelance hire

If none of the other options suits your project you can hire me for an hourly rate.
We will discuss of what your project needs and find out how much time it would take me in order to get the right price.

Price range : 30€ / hour
